Movie Summary

Judy Hopps hopes to be a police officer when she grows up for the city of Zootopia. She would be the first rabbit police officer for the city of Zootopia but must face many challenges on the way. She meets Nick Wilde and together help solve the mystery of missing mammals in this highly anticipated disney classic.
